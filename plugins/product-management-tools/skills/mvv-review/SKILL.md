# MVV Review

## Review Stance

Evaluate whether the MVV can actually guide decisions. Be direct about vague, interchangeable, or performative language.

## Checklist

- Mission: names the beneficiary, purpose, and enduring job.
- Vision: describes a distinct future state, not a near-term milestone.
- Values: describe observable behaviors and tradeoffs.
- Coherence: mission, vision, and values reinforce each other.
- Differentiation: language would not fit any random competitor.
- Usability: teams can use it to resolve product, hiring, brand, or prioritization decisions.

## Output

Lead with findings ordered by severity. For each finding, include the issue, why it matters, and a concrete rewrite or decision question. End with a tightened draft only when enough context exists.
