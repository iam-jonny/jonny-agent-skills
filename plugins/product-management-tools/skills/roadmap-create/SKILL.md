# Roadmap Create

## Workflow

1. Confirm the roadmap audience and horizon: leadership, product team, engineering, GTM, customers, or board.
2. Anchor the roadmap in outcomes and strategic themes before listing features.
3. Group work into now/next/later, quarters, milestones, or bets based on the user's planning style.
4. Show dependencies, confidence, risks, and decision points.
5. Avoid over-committing exact dates unless the user provides delivery constraints.

## Output

Return a roadmap with:

- Strategic themes
- Outcome targets
- Initiatives or bets
- Time horizon or sequencing
- Dependencies and constraints
- Risks and assumptions
- Decision checkpoints

## Quality Bar

Make the roadmap useful for tradeoffs. It should explain why work is sequenced, what can move, and what success means.
